A Photo Anthology:

Body of water

Body of Water brings together collections of photos
captured over the years, all on analog film and
interconnected by a single theme: exploring the
human body and water.

Each project documents a personal search for
presence and transformation, using the fluidity of
water as both a visual and emotional mirror.

The work is guided by a desire to express to the
world moments of beauty, tenderness, and
silent introspection.
